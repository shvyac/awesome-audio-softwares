## Music Visualization

- [projectM](https://github.com/projectM-visualizer/projectm) - Cross-platform, open-source music visualization library that is MilkDrop-compatible, rendering real-time audio-reactive visuals via OpenGL.
- [MilkDrop](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MilkDrop) - The classic hardware-accelerated music visualization plugin for Winamp, created by Ryan Geiss, known for its beat-synced, user-created "presets."
- [Advanced Visualization Studio (AVS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advanced_Visualization_Studio) - A music visualization plugin for Winamp by Nullsoft, allowing users to build custom visual effect chains.
- [G-Force](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G-Force_(visualization_software)) - A popular music visualization plugin by Andy O'Meara, known for its detailed, organic 3D visuals.
- [harmony_visualizer](https://github.com/Frieve-A/harmony_visualizer) - A tool by Frieve-A for visualizing the harmony of MIDI input.

## Audio Effects & Processing

- [EffeTune](https://github.com/Frieve-A/effetune) - A real-time audio effect processor by Frieve-A designed for audio enthusiasts to enhance their music listening experience.

## Audio Analysis & Tools

- [Frieve's Sound Toolbox](https://github.com/Frieve-A/sound_toolbox) - A collection of browser-based sound-related tools by Frieve-A, including a preview audio player, an ABX tester, and a digital sampling visualizer.
- [DIP Bench](https://github.com/Frieve-A/dipbench) - A digital piano benchmark tool by Frieve-A.
- [Audio Review](https://github.com/Frieve-A/audioreview) - Source for an audio equipment review site by Frieve-A providing objective evaluations of headphones, speakers, and amplifiers.
